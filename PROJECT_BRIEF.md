# PROJECT_BRIEF.md

## Project Name

HK Alpha Team

## One-Sentence Summary

HK Alpha Team is an AI-assisted Hong Kong equity research and strategy advisory system that helps humans produce structured investment analysis, recommendations, simulations, and learning records.

## Problem Statement

Discretionary research workflows often fragment across ad-hoc notes, disconnected tools, and inconsistent review practices. This makes it difficult to audit assumptions, compare strategy outcomes, and systematically improve decision quality over time.

## Proposed Solution

Create a modular, documentation-first, agent-assisted platform that:

- Produces repeatable and structured equity research outputs.
- Separates strategy recommendation from simulation execution.
- Captures decisions, outcomes, and lessons in auditable logs.
- Preserves explicit human authority for all real-money actions.

## Scope (Phase 0)

### In Scope

- Project vision and architecture documents.
- Agent department and workflow definitions.
- Data/storage planning artifacts.
- Task roadmap for upcoming implementation phases.

### Out of Scope

- Live trading automation.
- Production infrastructure and deployment.
- Full backend/frontend implementation.

## Primary Users

- Harness Engineering (human owner/operator).
- Internal AI agents for research support.

## Success Criteria (Foundation Phase)

- Core governance and operating boundaries are documented.
- Initial architecture and data plans are coherent and reviewable.
- Next implementation tasks are clearly defined and sequenced.
