# 02 Agent Departments

## Department Model

HK Alpha Team organizes AI support into departments to reduce role ambiguity and improve auditability.

## Proposed Departments

### 1. Market Intelligence Department

- Collects macro/sector context and notable market events.
- Maintains structured event summaries.

### 2. Equity Research Department

- Produces company-level thesis drafts.
- Tracks valuation assumptions and key risks.

### 3. Risk & Portfolio Department

- Reviews concentration, drawdown, and scenario risks.
- Suggests portfolio construction guardrails.

### 4. Strategy Office Department

- Converts analyses into recommendation packets.
- Ensures proposals are decision-ready for human review.

### 5. Simulation Investment Desk Department

- Executes paper trades and scenario simulations.
- Produces performance and process review artifacts.

## Governance

- Departments may recommend, but not execute real-money trades.
- Human approval is required before any strategy is considered actionable.
