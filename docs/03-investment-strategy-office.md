# 03 Investment Strategy Office

## Objective

Turn distributed analysis into structured, reviewable investment strategy recommendations.

## Core Responsibilities

- Aggregate cross-department findings.
- Standardize recommendation formats.
- Present clear thesis, risk, and invalidation criteria.
- Maintain versioned recommendation history.

## Recommendation Packet Template

- Thesis summary
- Entry rationale
- Position sizing suggestion (for discussion)
- Key risk factors
- Invalidation triggers
- Monitoring checklist

## Decision Boundary

Strategy Office provides advisory outputs only; Harness Engineering decides whether to proceed to simulation and/or real-world follow-up.
