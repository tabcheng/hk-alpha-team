# 00 Project Vision

## Vision

Build a trustworthy, iterative AI-assisted investment research environment focused on Hong Kong equities.

## Principles

1. Human decision authority is non-negotiable.
2. Advice quality is improved through structure, review, and iteration.
3. Simulation precedes confidence.
4. Every important assumption should be traceable.

## Desired Outcomes

- Consistent research memos and strategy proposals.
- Clear linkage between hypotheses and simulated outcomes.
- Measurable improvement loop through logs and retrospectives.

## Scope Boundary Reminder

HK Alpha Team does not execute real-money trades automatically.
