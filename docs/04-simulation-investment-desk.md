# 04 Simulation Investment Desk

## Core Role

The Simulation Investment Desk is a core learning and validation module. It evaluates recommendation quality through disciplined paper trading and structured post-outcome review.

The desk has two distinct simulation-origin pipelines:

1. `user_recorded` paper trades recorded by the human user / Harness Engineering.
2. `system_generated_learning` simulations generated by HK Alpha Team / the Simulation Investment Desk to validate recommendation quality and produce reviewable learning proposals.

The two origins must remain distinguishable in product contracts, schema planning, validation helpers, tests, reports, and future runtime/persistence work.

## Simulation-Origin Pipelines

### `user_recorded`

- **Origin:** Human user / Harness Engineering / user-entered record.
- **Purpose:** Record user-entered paper trading decisions, rationale, notes, linked recommendation when available, and outcome review fields.
- **Primary responsibility:** Preserve the human's paper-trade journal entry without implying that HK Alpha Team generated the simulation for learning validation.
- **Required source fields:** `simulation_origin = "user_recorded"`, `created_by_type`, user/source identifier, `user_recorded_notes`, user rationale, optional `strategy_recommendation_id` when a human links the trade to a recommendation, and trade review fields when the outcome is reviewed.
- **Learning impact:** May inform later human-reviewed lessons, but does not automatically create or apply system-generated learning proposals unless explicitly linked and reviewed.

### `system_generated_learning`

- **Origin:** Simulation Investment Desk / HK Alpha Team system.
- **Purpose:** Validate strategy recommendation quality through disciplined paper trading and post-outcome review.
- **Primary responsibility:** Preserve the original recommendation packet and simulation assumptions so the desk can learn from wins, losses, and invalidated theses without rewriting history.
- **Required linkage fields:** `simulation_origin = "system_generated_learning"`, `created_by_type`, `source_recommendation_id` or `strategy_recommendation_id`, original recommendation label, original scores, original thesis, entry assumptions, exit assumptions, PnL, holding period, what worked, what failed, improvement suggestions, `system_learning_reason`, and `requires_human_review`.
- **Learning impact:** May create `learning_proposals`, but proposals are reviewable only and must not be silently auto-applied to production strategy logic.

## Mandatory Recorded Fields

The simulation system records:

- Simulation origin: `user_recorded` or `system_generated_learning`
- Created-by/source metadata
- Original recommendation, if linked
- Original scores, for system-generated learning simulations
- Original thesis, for system-generated learning simulations
- Simulated entry price
- Simulated exit price
- Realized and unrealized PnL
- Holding period
- Outcome review
- What worked
- What failed
- Improvement suggestions
- User-recorded notes, when entered by Harness Engineering
- System-learning reason, when generated by the Simulation Investment Desk
- Human-review requirement for learning proposals

## Operating Rules

1. Historical recommendations must not be overwritten to make results look better.
2. Losing paper trades and losing system-generated simulations must remain visible in reports.
3. Simulation results do not guarantee real-world performance.
4. The simulation system must not execute real-money trades.
5. The simulation system must not connect to broker execution APIs for autonomous order placement.
6. Strategy learning should produce reviewable proposals, not silently change production logic.
7. User-recorded paper trades and system-generated learning simulations must remain distinguishable and auditable.

## Output Artifacts

- User-recorded paper trade journal entries
- System-generated learning simulation records
- Paper trade blotter
- Outcome and performance summaries
- Post-simulation review notes
- Proposed strategy/process improvements for human review
- Audit events preserving origin, actor, and learning-proposal status

## Boundary Reminder

Simulation outputs are advisory and educational. Final real-money decisions remain with the user / Harness Engineering. Task 008G opens only non-real-money productization gates for contract/schema alignment and local validation; endpoint runtime, persistence writes, production Supabase connections, live market data, broker execution APIs, deployment, and real-money trading remain out of scope until separately approved.
