# 04 Simulation Investment Desk

## Objective

Provide disciplined paper-trading and simulation workflows to evaluate strategy robustness before any real-money consideration.

## Responsibilities

- Receive approved strategy packets from Strategy Office.
- Execute paper trades in simulation environment.
- Record assumptions, timestamps, and simulated fills.
- Produce periodic performance and process reviews.

## Output Artifacts

- Trade blotter (paper only)
- Strategy performance snapshots
- Post-simulation review notes
- Improvement proposals

## Guardrails

- No connection to broker order execution.
- No claim that simulation guarantees real-world performance.
- All findings are advisory and educational.
